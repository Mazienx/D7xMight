Bite by Bytes

"The food is an illusion. Bite in — and find the matrix."

In a compound in Lagos, a young boy works hard every single day — and still has nothing to show for it. Not because he is lazy. But because no system was designed to catch his effort and return it as something visible.

"I worked today. I worked yesterday. I worked the day before. So why does it feel like I haven't moved at all?"

Bite by Bytes is a micro-work platform being built for young people, students, and minors who have a phone, willingness to contribute, and nowhere for their effort to go. Small tasks. Real pay. Visible growth. Built on full transparency and a public safeguarding agreement that protects every minor inside the system.
