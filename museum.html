ABOUT ART GALAXY

97 Art Galaxy is an interdimensional art platform that transcends traditional exhibition spaces. Founded in 2023 by a collective of astrophysicists and avant-garde artists, we've created a revolutionary approach to experiencing art across multiple sensory dimensions.

  • Immersive exhibitions blending physical and digital realities
